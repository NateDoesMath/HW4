Popular Tools for VR


There are many different ways to develope for VR softwares.

However, the most popular will be listed here.


One of these tools is Unity 3D. At its heart, it is a game engine, but other software can be developed using it.

It uses the languages of C# and Java, so programmers with experience with those languages should feel comfortable.


Another major tool used is Unreal Enginge 4. It is also a gaming engine like Unity, but the graphics are more advanced.

and realistic. It is the main competitor to Unity, and it uses C++ for it's scripting language.


The third most popular is Autodesk's 3DS Max and Maya tool. They are used for modeling, animation, lighing, and VFX.

While they do not have VR support by default, they do have plugins to aquire the support for it.

It is mainly used in the architectural design industry.


There are many other softwares for VR, but Unity and Unreal Engine are by far the most popular.
