Uses of VR


Everyone knows that video games are the most popular use for VR, but there are other uses that are not as talked about.

Believe it or not the millitary is using VR technology for training their soldiers.

The US and the UK have both adopted it's use for training, which allows for a huge selection of simulations to put recruits through.


Another use is the use for sports. VR is being used by coaches and players to train more efficiently.

It is also being used by fans to watch the games as if they were in the stands or on the field of play.

There are plans to one day sell virtual tickets to watch these games live from your living room.


VR is also being used in mental health and the medical field. Due to the interactive nature of VR, it is a popular tool

used for treating anxiety, phobias, and depression. It allows a person to reinact a traumatic event to help them come to

terms with the event and heal. It is also being used for practicing surgeries and mediacal procedures. It provides the ability

to make mistakes without there being risk to a real person.


It is also being used in education as well. Students are able to interact with each other in a virtual 3D environment and

can be taken on virtual field trips. This is especially useful right now during the COVID-19 pandemic. It makes learning exciting

when it would normally be boring and dull.
